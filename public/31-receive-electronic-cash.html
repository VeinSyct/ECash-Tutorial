Tutorials - Receive Electronic Cash

To receive electronic cash, a sender must transfer it to your electronic cash address. You can share your address using a QR code displayed on your device screen.


Step 1

Display your electronic cash address QR code on your device for the benefactor to scan.



Step 2

After the benefactor acquires your electronic cash address via QR code, they can specify the amount of electronic cash to transfer to you. A transfer QR code will then appear on the benefactor's device screen for you to scan. Scan the code until a confirmation dialog appears, indicating that the electronic cash transfer has been successful.



Step 3

After completing the QR code scan for the electronic cash transfer, check your balance.
