Tutorials - Receive Electronic Cash

Topic 3.1

For a beneficiary to receive electronic cash, the benefactor must transfer it to the beneficiary's electronic cash address. The beneficiary can share the electronic cash address using a QR code displayed onto the device screen.


Step 1

Display user's (beneficiary is you) electronic cash address QR code onto the device screen for the benefactor to scan.



Step 2

After the benefactor acquires the beneficiary's electronic cash address, the benefactor can specify the currency and the amount of electronic cash to transfer to you. A transfer QR code will then appear on the benefactor's device screen for you to scan. Scan the code until a confirmation dialog appears, indicating that the electronic cash transfer has been successful.



Step 3

After completing the QR code scan for the electronic cash transfer, check your balance.
